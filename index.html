Our SDG Project

Our project focuses on SDG #4: Quality Education. We aim to make coding accessible for everyone by creating interactive, creative, and inclusive learning platforms for youth.

Through our IamTheCode journey, we developed projects promoting equal opportunities in technology education and empowering young learners to become digital creators.
